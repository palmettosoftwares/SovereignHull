Common Questions

What is marine PPF, exactly?

A quick primer if this is new to you — most boat owners have heard of ceramic coating, fewer have heard of film.

1

What is paint protection film?

A thin, optically clear polyurethane film applied directly to your hull and other high-wear surfaces. It's a physical barrier, not a chemical coating — it takes the impact, UV exposure, and abrasion so your gelcoat doesn't have to.

2

How is this different from wax or ceramic coating?

Wax and ceramic coatings are chemical sealants — they add shine and some protection, but they're microns thick and wear off. Film has real physical thickness, so it protects against dock rash, scratches, and impact the way a coating simply can't.

3

Will it change how my boat looks?

No — high-quality film is optically clear and designed to preserve your factory finish, not alter it. Once installed, it's virtually invisible.

4

How long does an install take, and how long does it last?

Timeline and lifespan both depend on your boat's size and which package you choose — we'll walk through specifics for your exact boat during your consultation rather than guess here.
