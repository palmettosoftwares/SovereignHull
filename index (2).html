Home  /  Services

Film protection, applied with precision.

Every install is fitted panel by panel to your hull's actual contours — not stretched, not guessed at.

01

Full Hull Wraps

Complete edge-to-edge coverage that shields gelcoat from UV fade, oxidation, and everyday wear. This is the most comprehensive layer of protection we offer, built for boats that spend every day on the water.

02

Outboard Motor Protection

Film protection for cowlings and motor housings — guarding against dock rash, prop scratches, exhaust residue, and UV fade so your motors look as sharp as the day you bought them.

03

Bow & Impact Zones

Reinforced protection where docking, debris, and chop hit hardest — the first line of defense against the dings and scuffs that add up over a season.

04

Waterline & Transom

Targeted film application on the surfaces that take the most constant abrasion and staining, keeping the lines of your boat looking clean season after season.

05

Interior Protection

PPF for helm surfaces, consoles, and high-touch interior points that see constant sun exposure and daily wear — protecting the parts of your boat you touch every time you're aboard.

06

Care & Maintenance

Seasonal inspections and film upkeep to keep your protection performing at its best, year after year.

Home  /  Process

Four steps. No shortcuts.

The same disciplined sequence on a weekend cruiser as on the boat you take out once a year.

01

Consultation

We walk your boat with you, note trouble spots, and scope exactly what needs protecting — so there are no surprises once the work begins.

02

Detail & Prep

Surfaces are fully cleaned, decontaminated, and corrected before a single panel is cut. Protection is only as good as the surface underneath it.

03

Precision Install

Film is fitted and set to your hull's exact curves, with seams hidden along natural lines wherever possible.

04

Final Inspection

Every edge checked, every panel confirmed, before we call the job done.
